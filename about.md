# About

Zethesis Research & Engineering is a private R&D initiative founded by Robert Smith, PhD.

The focus is on advanced simulation, numerical methods and scientific software for industrial engineering applications.

Zethesis bridges rigorous research and industrial practice by working on CFD workflows, validation, scientific computing and research-code industrialization.

The Zethesis mark is inspired by aquatic birds such as the kingfisher and the heron: forms shaped by air, water, precision and patience.

The kingfisher suggests fast penetration of a fluid interface with minimal disturbance. The heron suggests balance, observation and controlled motion. Both reflect the same idea behind our work: understanding flow, reducing complexity, and turning physics into engineering insight.
